Roast Pork

Description

A roast pork recipe that is sure to be a hit with everyone, especially the cook as the slow cooker makes this dish easy to make and clean up! It can easily be topped with gravy made from the juices of this roast. Favorite sides for this dish include green beans, corn, or your choice of greens!

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
  • 1 (3 pound) pork sirloin roast
  • 1 (16 ounce) can whole berry cranberry sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rub soup mix all over pork.
  2. Place roast in a slow cooker and pour cranberry sauce over top.
  3. Cover and cook on low until pork is slightly pink in the center, about 8 to 10 hours or until roast reaches internal temperature of 145 degrees F.
  4. Transfer roast to a separate dish, reserving liquids in the slow cooker.
  5. Melt butter in a sauce pan over low heat.
  6. Whisk in flour to form a paste. Pour in reserved juices from the slow cooker, whisking constantly, until gravy lightens in color and thickens about 5 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Serve alongside roast.
